<p><p>The bonded abrasive market is categorized into two main types: natural and synthetic abrasives. Natural abrasives, such as garnet and emery, are sourced directly from nature and are known for their unique properties. Synthetic abrasives, on the other hand, are manufactured using chemical processes to create materials like aluminum oxide and silicon carbide, which can be tailored for specific applications.
