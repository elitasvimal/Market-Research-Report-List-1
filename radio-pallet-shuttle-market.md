<p><p>In the Radio Pallet Shuttle market, FIFO (First In, First Out) and LIFO (Last In, First Out) are two inventory management methods. FIFO ensures that older stock is used or sold before newer stock, promoting freshness and minimizing waste, ideal for perishable goods. Conversely, LIFO prioritizes the most recently added inventory for picking, which can help in cash flow management by keeping older inventory on hand. Both methods cater to different operational needs depending on product type and market demand.</p></p>
