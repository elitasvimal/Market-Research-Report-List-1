<p><p>The food pouch film market comprises various types that cater to different packaging needs. Aluminum foil offers excellent barrier properties and is often used for moisture-sensitive products. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film is known for its clarity and strength, ideal for snacks. BOPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) film provides high oxygen barrier for meat and dairy. BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) film offers durability and thermal resistance. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film is flexible and sealable, while LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) film provides toughness and elasticity. Other films may include specialized composites or biodegradable options for niche applications.</p></p>
