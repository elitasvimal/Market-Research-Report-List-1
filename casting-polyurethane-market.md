<p><p>The casting polyurethane market comprises three primary types of prepolymers: Polyester Base Prepolymer, Polyether Base Prepolymer, and Polycaprolactone Base Prepolymer. Polyester-based prepolymers offer excellent mechanical properties and thermal resistance, making them suitable for industrial applications. Polyether-based prepolymers provide enhanced flexibility and resistance to hydrolysis, ideal for varied applications in footwear and coatings. Polycaprolactone-based prepolymers exhibit superior elasticity and biodegradability, catering to niche markets like medical devices and environmentally friendly products, thus diversifying the application landscape.</p></p>
