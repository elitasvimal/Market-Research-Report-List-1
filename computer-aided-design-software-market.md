<p><p>The Computer Aided Design (CAD) software market can be categorized into two main types: On-Premise and On-Cloud. On-Premise CAD software is installed and operated locally on individual or company servers, providing users with full control over data and security but requiring ongoing maintenance and updates. Conversely, On-Cloud CAD software is hosted on external servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates, making it ideal for collaboration and remote work environments without the hardware investment.</p></p>
