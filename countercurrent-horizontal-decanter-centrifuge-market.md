<p><p>The Countercurrent Horizontal Decanter Centrifuge market is primarily categorized into two segments: two-phase and three-phase systems. The two-phase decanter efficiently separates solids from liquids, ideal for applications like wastewater treatment and slurry processing. In contrast, the three-phase decanter simultaneously separates solids and two immiscible liquids, commonly used in industries such as food processing and oil recovery. Both types leverage centrifugal force to enhance separation efficiency, catering to distinct industrial processing needs and applications.</p></p>
