<p><p>The polysilicon special transformer market is primarily segmented into two types: dry type and liquid filling type. Dry type transformers utilize air as a cooling medium, making them suitable for indoor applications and environments where fire safety is a concern. In contrast, liquid filling type transformers employ insulating oil to facilitate better heat dissipation, allowing for higher power ratings and outdoor installation. Both types play crucial roles in supporting the energy needs of polysilicon production processes in the solar industry.</p></p>
