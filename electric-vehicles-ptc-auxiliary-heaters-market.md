<p><p>The Electric Vehicles PTC Auxiliary Heaters market comprises three main types: immersion heaters, radiator heaters, and pad-style heaters. </p><p>Immersion heaters directly heat coolant in a tank, ensuring rapid temperature rise. Radiator heaters utilize the vehicle's cooling system to distribute heat, providing efficient warmth throughout the cabin. Pad-style heaters are installed under seats or floors, delivering direct warmth to occupants. Together, these technologies enhance passenger comfort in electric vehicles, facilitating optimal performance even in colder climates by maintaining interior temperatures without relying solely on battery power.</p></p>
