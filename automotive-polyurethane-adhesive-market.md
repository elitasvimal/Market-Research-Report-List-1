<p><ul><li>One-component Adhesive</li><li>Two-component Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive polyurethane adhesive market consists of two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-formulated, requiring no mixing before application, making them easy to use and ideal for quick repairs. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed prior to application, offering superior bonding strength and durability. Both types are essential in vehicle manufacturing and repair, primarily used for bonding components like windshields, interiors, and structural elements.</p></p>
