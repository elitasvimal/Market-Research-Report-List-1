<p><p>The needleless injectors market encompasses applications for intradermal, subcutaneous, and intramuscular injections, allowing for efficient drug delivery without traditional needles. Intradermal injections are used for allergy testing and vaccinations, facilitating precise medication administration into the skin's upper layer. Subcutaneous injections target the fatty tissue just beneath the skin, ideal for insulin and vaccines, promoting slower absorption. Intramuscular injections deliver medication deeper into muscle tissues, ensuring faster absorption, commonly used for antibiotics and hormonal therapies. This technology enhances patient comfort and safety.</p></p>
