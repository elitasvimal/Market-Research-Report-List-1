<p><p>The water filters market can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Reverse Osmosis Water Filters**: These systems use a semipermeable membrane to remove contaminants, offering thorough purification by tackling dissolved solids, heavy metals, and microorganisms for cleaner drinking water.</p><p>2. **Ultrafiltration Membrane Water Filters**: These filters utilize a membrane that separates larger particles, such as bacteria and viruses, from water, providing effective filtration without the need for chemicals.</p><p>3. **Other Water Filters**: This category includes various filtration methods like activated carbon, ceramic, and UV filters, catering to diverse consumer needs based on specific water quality issues.</p></p>
