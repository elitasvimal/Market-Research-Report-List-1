<p><p>The stage micrometer market is categorized into two main types: linear stage micrometers and rotary stage micrometers. Linear stage micrometers are primarily used for precise measurement along a straight path, making them ideal for applications in microscopy and calibration. In contrast, rotary stage micrometers facilitate measurement and adjustment in rotational movements, commonly utilized in optical and mechanical systems. Both types are essential for enhancing measurement accuracy in laboratory and industrial settings, driving demand in various scientific fields.</p></p>
