<p><p>The ceramic-based lead zirconate titanate (PZT) market is categorized into two main types: hard PZT and soft PZT. Hard PZT is characterized by its high piezoelectric coefficients and is commonly used in applications requiring strong mechanical rigidity, such as sensors and actuators. In contrast, soft PZT has lower mechanical stiffness but exhibits higher electromechanical coupling, making it ideal for applications like transducers and high-frequency devices. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, including electronics, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
