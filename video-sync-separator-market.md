<p><strong>The Video Sync Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Composite</li><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Video Sync Separator market can be categorized into three main types based on signal processing: Composite, Horizontal, and Vertical separators. Composite separators extract synchronization signals from a composite video signal, which includes luminance and chrominance. Horizontal separators focus on distinguishing horizontal line synchronization, ensuring accurate line timing. Vertical separators target vertical synchronization, maintaining frame integrity. Each type plays a crucial role in video processing, serving various applications in broadcasting, security systems, and multimedia devices, enhancing overall video quality and performance.</p></p>
