<p><p>The civil aircraft jacks market comprises various types, primarily including tripod jacks and axle jacks. Tripod jacks are designed for lifting fuselages, providing stability and versatility during maintenance. They feature a three-legged design that distributes weight evenly and enhances safety. Axle jacks, on the other hand, are specifically tailored for lifting aircraft wheels or landing gear, facilitating tire changes and mechanical inspections. Both types of jacks are essential for ensuring efficient aircraft servicing and safety in aviation operations.</p></p>
