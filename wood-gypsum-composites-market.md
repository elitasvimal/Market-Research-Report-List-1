<p><p>The Wood Gypsum Composites market is categorized into two main types: Monolayer and Multi-storey Wood Gypsum Composites. Monolayer composites consist of a single layer of wood and gypsum, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for applications like flooring and paneling. Both types leverage the sustainable benefits of wood and the durability of gypsum, catering to diverse architectural needs.</p></p>
