<p><p>The mechanical thrombectomy devices market includes various types designed for clot removal in patients with vascular obstructions. Ultrasonic thrombectomy devices utilize sound waves to break down clots, while rheolytic devices employ high-velocity saline jets to fragment them. General mechanical thrombectomy devices use mechanical force for extraction, whereas aspiration thrombectomy devices create suction to remove clots. Other types may include novel or hybrid approaches. Each device type offers unique mechanisms, catering to different patient needs and clinical situations in vascular interventions.</p></p>
