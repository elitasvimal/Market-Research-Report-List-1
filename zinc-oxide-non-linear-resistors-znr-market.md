SMD ZNRs are compact, suitable for modern PCB designs, and enable automated assembly, making them ideal for high-density applications. Leaded ZNRs, on the other hand, are often used in traditional through-hole designs, providing robust performance for various power electronics applications. Both types play essential roles in ensuring circuit reliability and safety.</p></p>
