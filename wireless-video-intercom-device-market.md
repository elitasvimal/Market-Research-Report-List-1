<p><p>The wireless video intercom device market encompasses three main types: Door Stations, Video Intercom Masters, and Indoor Units. Door Stations are the exterior components that capture and transmit video and audio from visitors, enhancing security. Video Intercom Masters serve as central control units, allowing users to manage communication and video feeds from multiple Door Stations. Indoor Units are installed inside the building, enabling residents to interact with visitors conveniently, monitor entry points, and communicate without opening doors.</p></p>
