<p><p>The air tightness detector market comprises various types, primarily including differential pressure and direct pressure types. Differential pressure detectors assess air tightness by measuring the pressure difference between two points, indicating leaks or faults. In contrast, direct pressure detectors measure the absolute air pressure within a space to determine tightness levels directly. Other market segments may include advanced methods or hybrid technologies that combine features of both types, catering to diverse applications in building compliance and energy efficiency assessments.</p></p>
