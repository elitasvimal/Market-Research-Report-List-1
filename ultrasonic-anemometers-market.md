<p><p>The ultrasonic anemometer market comprises two primary types: two-dimensional and three-dimensional sonic anemometers. Two-dimensional sonic anemometers measure wind speed across two axes, providing data on horizontal wind components. In contrast, three-dimensional sonic anemometers measure wind velocity in three dimensions, capturing detailed information about wind speed and direction in a volumetric space. Both types utilize ultrasonic sound waves, allowing for precise and real-time wind measurements, making them essential for meteorological research, environmental monitoring, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
