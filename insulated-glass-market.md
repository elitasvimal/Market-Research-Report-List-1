<p><p>The insulated glass market features various types designed for enhanced performance. Low-emission (Low-E) glass reduces heat transfer while maintaining visibility. Fully tempered glass provides increased strength and safety through a specialized heating process. Soft coat Low-E glass features a thin metallic layer for superior insulation, while hard coat Low-E glass offers durability with a more robust surface. Clear annealed thickness ranges from 1/8” to 1/4” for standard insulation needs, while clear laminated glass provides added strength. Bulletproof glass, made with multiple layers, ensures security and protection.</p></p>
