<p><strong>The Medical Grade Elastomeric Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset Elastomeric Materials</li><li>Thermoplastic Elastomeric Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical grade elastomeric materials market is divided into two main types: thermoset elastomeric materials and thermoplastic elastomeric materials. Thermoset elastomers are characterized by their irreversible curing process, resulting in a durable and heat-resistant structure, ideal for applications like seals and gaskets. In contrast, thermoplastic elastomers are flexible and can be repeatedly softened and reshaped through heating, making them suitable for products like tubing and containers. Both types play crucial roles in medical devices, providing essential properties such as biocompatibility and flexibility.</p></p>
