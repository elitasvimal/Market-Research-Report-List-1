<p><p>The nuclear steam generator market includes two primary types: horizontal steam generators and vertical steam generators. Horizontal steam generators are designed with horizontal tubes, facilitating easy maintenance and efficient heat exchange, making them suitable for various reactor types. In contrast, vertical steam generators feature vertical tubes, optimizing space and enhancing thermal efficiency, often preferred in modern reactors. Both types serve the critical function of transferring heat from the reactor coolant to produce steam for power generation, catering to different operational needs and design preferences.</p></p>
