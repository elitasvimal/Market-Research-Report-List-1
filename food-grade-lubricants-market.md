Synthetic oils are man-made and offer superior performance, stability, and resistance to extreme temperatures, making them ideal for high-demand applications. Bio-based oils are derived from renewable resources, providing an eco-friendly alternative that meets safety standards while delivering effective lubrication for food processing operations.</p></p>
