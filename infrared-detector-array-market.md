<p><p>The infrared detector array market includes various types such as indium antimonide (InSb), lead selenide (PbSe), lead sulfide (PbS), and others. InSb detectors are known for their high sensitivity in the mid-infrared range, making them ideal for applications like thermal imaging. PbSe detectors are sensitive to longer wavelengths, suited for gas detection. PbS detectors are often used in low-cost applications. Other types may include quantum dots and microbolometers, catering to diverse needs across industries such as defense, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
