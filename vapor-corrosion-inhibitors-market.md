<p><strong>The Vapor Corrosion Inhibitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anode Inhibitor</li><li>Cathode Inhibitor</li><li>Mixed Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vapor Corrosion Inhibitors (VCIs) market is categorized into three types: anode inhibitors, cathode inhibitors, and mixed inhibitors. Anode inhibitors function by forming a protective layer on the anode surfaces to prevent corrosion. Cathode inhibitors, on the other hand, target cathodic surfaces, providing protection through a different mechanism. Mixed inhibitors offer dual protection by addressing both anode and cathode corrosion processes. These VCIs are essential in various industries for prolonging the lifespan of metal products by mitigating corrosion.</p></p>
