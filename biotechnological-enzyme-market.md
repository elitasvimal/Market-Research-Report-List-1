<p><p>The biotechnological enzyme market can be categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Hydrolases**: Enzymes that catalyze the breakdown of substrates by hydrolysis, crucial in food and biofuel industries.</p><p>2. **Lyases**: Enzymes that break chemical bonds without water, essential for metabolic processes.</p><p>3. **Isomerases**: Enzymes that facilitate intramolecular rearrangements, important in carbohydrate and amino acid metabolism.</p><p>4. **Ligases**: Enzymes that join two molecules using ATP, vital for DNA replication and repair.</p><p>5. **Others**: This category includes various specialized enzymes not classified above, servicing niche applications across industries.</p></p>
