<p><p>The horizontal bowl centrifuge market is divided into two primary types: two-phase and three-phase centrifuges. Two-phase centrifuges separate solid and liquid components from a mixture, primarily used in wastewater treatment and food processing. In contrast, three-phase centrifuges simultaneously separate three components—typically two immiscible liquids and solids—making them ideal for applications like oil and gas extraction or dairy processing. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing their design, operation, and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
