<p><p>The Therapeutic Drug Monitoring (TDM) market consists of various testing types to measure drug levels in the body, ensuring effective therapy while minimizing toxicity. Blood tests, the most common, assess drug concentration directly from the bloodstream. Urine tests evaluate drug metabolites, providing insights into overall drug clearance. Saliva tests offer a non-invasive alternative, reflecting free drug levels. Other tests may include specialized assays or analytical techniques, broadening the scope of monitoring to cater to diverse therapeutic needs and patient populations.</p></p>
