<p><p>Immersion parts washers are categorized into two main types: solvent parts washers and aqueous parts washers. Solvent parts washers utilize chemical solvents for cleaning, effectively removing oils and greases but may pose environmental and health risks due to volatile organic compounds. Aqueous parts washers, on the other hand, use water-based solutions, often combined with detergents, making them more environmentally friendly. Both types serve various industries, ensuring thorough cleaning of components without damaging the parts being cleaned.</p></p>
