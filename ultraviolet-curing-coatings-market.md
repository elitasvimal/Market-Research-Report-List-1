<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) curing coatings encompass various market types, primarily water-based and powder-based formulations. Water-based UV coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering low emissions and reduced environmental impact while providing excellent adhesion and flexibility. Powder-based UV coatings involve the application of a dry powder that cures upon UV exposure, resulting in a durable finish with minimal solvent use. Both types are favored for their quick curing times, efficiency, and versatility across diverse applications, enhancing sustainability in the coatings industry.</p></p>
