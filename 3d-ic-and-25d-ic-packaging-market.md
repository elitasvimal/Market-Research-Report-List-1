<p><strong>The 3D IC and 25D IC Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3D Wafer-level Chip-scale Packaging</li><li>3D TSV</li><li>2.5D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D IC packaging integrates multiple chips vertically, enhancing performance by reducing interconnect lengths. Key types include 3D wafer-level chip-scale packaging, which offers compact designs using multiple dies in a single package for improved thermal and performance efficiency. 3D TSV (Through-Silicon Via) technology connects chips vertically through silicon, ensuring high data transfer rates. The 2.5D market uses an interposer to place multiple chips side by side, facilitating connectivity and power efficiency, bridging the gap between traditional 2D and 3D packaging.</p></p>
