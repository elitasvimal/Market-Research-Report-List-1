<p><p>The High Performance Polyurethane Elastomers market is segmented into Thermoset PU Elastomers and Thermoplastic PU Elastomers. Thermoset PU Elastomers are characterized by their rigid structure and are typically cross-linked, providing excellent chemical resistance and high temperature stability. They are commonly used in applications requiring durability and toughness. In contrast, Thermoplastic PU Elastomers are flexible, can be repeatedly melted and reshaped, making them ideal for applications needing versatility and ease of processing, such as in automotive and consumer goods.</p></p>
