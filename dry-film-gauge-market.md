<p><strong>The Dry Film Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Coating Thickness Gauges</li><li>Mechanical Coating Thickness Gauges</li><li>Destructive Coating Thickness Gauges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry film gauge market encompasses various types of coating thickness measurement tools. Digital coating thickness gauges provide precise, instant readings through electronic sensors, suitable for various surfaces. Mechanical coating thickness gauges utilize mechanical means, often featuring dial indicators, to measure film thickness; they are generally more portable but less precise. Destructive coating thickness gauges involve techniques that require removing a sample to measure thickness, offering accurate data but potentially damaging the substrate. Each type caters to specific industry needs and precision requirements.</p></p>
