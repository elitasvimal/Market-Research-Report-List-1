<p><strong>The Vibration Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Vibration Meter</li><li>Mechanical Vibration Meter</li><li>Table Frame Vibration Meter</li><li>Sponge Vibration Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration meters market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Piezoelectric vibration meters utilize piezoelectric crystals to convert mechanical motion into electrical signals, making them suitable for high-frequency measurements. Mechanical vibration meters are based on mass-spring systems, ideal for low-frequency monitoring. Table frame vibration meters are designed for stability and accuracy on flat surfaces, while sponge vibration meters use a soft base to absorb vibrations, offering versatility in various environments. Each type addresses different measurement needs effectively.</p></p>
