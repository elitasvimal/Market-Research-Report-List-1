<p><ul><li>Safety Couplings for Indirect Drives</li><li>Safety Couplings for Direct Drives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety couplings market is divided into two main types: those designed for indirect drives and those for direct drives. Safety couplings for indirect drives are used to protect machinery by disconnecting when excess torque is detected, preventing damage in applications like conveyor systems. In contrast, safety couplings for direct drives offer immediate disengagement in high-torque situations, commonly found in electric motors or high-speed machinery. Both types enhance operational safety and equipment longevity across various industries.</p></p>
