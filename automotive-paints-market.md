<p><p>The automotive paints market is categorized into several types. Solvent-borne paints, which contain organic solvents, offer excellent durability and gloss but raise environmental concerns. Water-borne paints use water as a solvent, providing lower VOC emissions and better environmental compliance. Powder paints, applied electrostatically and cured under heat, are eco-friendly and yield a durable finish. Other types may include specialty coatings and primers, each designed for specific applications, ensuring a variety of options for automotive manufacturers to meet performance and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
