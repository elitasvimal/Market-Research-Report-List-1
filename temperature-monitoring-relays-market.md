<p><p>Temperature monitoring relays are essential devices used to regulate and safeguard equipment by detecting temperature anomalies. The market comprises two primary types: overtemperature monitoring and undertemperature monitoring. Overtemperature monitoring relays activate alarms or shut down systems when temperatures exceed a predetermined threshold, preventing overheating and potential damage. Conversely, undertemperature monitoring relays ensure that temperatures do not fall below critical levels, which can be vital for processes requiring specific thermal conditions. Together, these monitoring solutions enhance safety and operational efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
