<p><strong>The Intrauterine Contraceptive Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hormonal IUCD</li><li>Copper IUCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intrauterine contraceptive device (IUCD) market comprises primarily two types: hormonal IUCDs and copper IUCDs. Hormonal IUCDs release progestin, which thickens cervical mucus and thins the uterine lining, providing effective pregnancy prevention and offering additional benefits like reduced menstrual bleeding. Copper IUCDs utilize copper ions to create an environment that is toxic to sperm, preventing fertilization. Both types cater to varying preferences for contraceptive methods, with hormonal IUCDs often preferred for their added health benefits, while copper IUCDs are favored for hormone-free contraception.</p></p>
