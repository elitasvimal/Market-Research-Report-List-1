<p><strong>The APF Active Power Filter for Electric Car Chargers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Active Power Filter</li><li>Series Active Power Filter</li><li>Hybrid Active Power Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Power Filters (APF) are crucial for enhancing power quality in electric car chargers. Shunt Active Power Filters are connected parallelly to the load, compensating for reactive power and harmonics. Series Active Power Filters are connected in series with the supply, effectively controlling voltage distortions and harmonics. Hybrid Active Power Filters combine the features of both shunt and series types, offering enhanced performance and flexibility. Together, they improve efficiency and stability in the electric vehicle charging infrastructure.</p></p>
