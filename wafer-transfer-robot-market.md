<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Manipulator</li><li>Vacuum Manipulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer transfer robot market is segmented into two main types: atmospheric manipulators and vacuum manipulators. Atmospheric manipulators operate in standard air conditions, using mechanical systems to handle wafers safely. They are typically suitable for less sensitive applications where contamination risk is minimal. In contrast, vacuum manipulators maintain a controlled environment by operating under vacuum conditions, which effectively minimizes particle contamination, making them ideal for high-precision semiconductor manufacturing processes where wafer integrity is critical.</p></p>
