<p><p>The vehicle insurance market encompasses treaty and facultative reinsurance as crucial components of risk management. Facultative reinsurance, on the other hand, is negotiated on a case-by-case basis for individual policies or risks, allowing insurers to seek additional coverage for specific high-risk vehicles.
