<p><p>The SLS (Selective Laser Sintering) machine market is primarily divided into two types: solid laser and gas laser. Solid lasers utilize solid-state technology, offering high precision and efficiency, making them suitable for a variety of high-performance applications. In contrast, gas lasers are often more cost-effective and versatile, commonly used for larger-scale production. Both types serve distinct industry needs, with solid lasers excelling in detailed prototypes and gas lasers facilitating broader manufacturing demands. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing additive manufacturing technologies.</p></p>
