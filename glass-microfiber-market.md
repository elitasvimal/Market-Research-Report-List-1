<p><strong>The Glass Microfiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Glass</li><li>B-Glass</li><li>C-Glass</li><li>E-Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass microfiber types are categorized based on their composition and properties. **A-Glass** (alkali glass) is common, used in general applications due to its durability and cost-effectiveness. **B-Glass** (borosilicate glass) offers high thermal and chemical resistance, ideal for labware and industrial uses. **C-Glass** (chemical glass) excels in corrosion resistance, often used in chemical environments. **E-Glass** (electrical glass) is the most widely used, known for its excellent electrical insulation and strength, making it a staple in electronics, composites, and construction. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
