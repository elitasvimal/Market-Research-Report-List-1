<p><ul><li>Passive Drains</li><li>Active Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wound drainage market is categorized into passive and active drainage systems. Passive drains utilize gravity to facilitate fluid removal, relying on natural fluid flow without any suction, making them simple and cost-effective. Conversely, active drains employ suction mechanisms, enhancing fluid evacuation from the wound site and reducing the risk of fluid accumulation, thereby promoting faster healing. Both types serve critical roles in postoperative care, addressing infection prevention and optimizing recovery outcomes in medical settings.</p></p>
