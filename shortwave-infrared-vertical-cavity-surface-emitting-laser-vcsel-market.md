<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser (VCSEL) market is divided into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode. </p><p>Single-mode VCSELs emit light through a single spatial mode, ensuring high coherence and precision, making them ideal for applications in telecommunications and sensing where long-distance transmission is required. In contrast, multi-mode VCSELs emit light through multiple spatial modes, providing greater output power and efficiency, often used in short-distance applications such as data centers and consumer electronics where broad bandwidth is beneficial.</p></p>
