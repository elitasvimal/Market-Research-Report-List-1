<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Acting Electromagnetic Valves</li><li>Sub-Step Direct-Acting Electromagnetic Valves</li><li>Pilot-Type Electromagnetic Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic valves market consists of three primary types. Direct-acting electromagnetic valves operate directly on the fluid flow with a solenoid aiding immediate activation. Sub-step direct-acting electromagnetic valves provide finer control and precision in fluid regulation by using multiple steps of actuation. Pilot-type electromagnetic valves involve a smaller actuating solenoid controlling a larger fluid flow, enabling efficient operation for high-pressure applications. Each type is tailored for specific industries, including automotive, HVAC, and manufacturing, based on their functionality and efficiency.</p></p>
