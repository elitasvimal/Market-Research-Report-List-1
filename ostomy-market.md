<p><strong>The Ostomy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colostomy</li><li>Ileostomy</li><li>Urostomy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ostomy market is segmented into three primary types: colostomy, ileostomy, and urostomy. Colostomy involves creating an opening in the abdomen for stool to exit from the colon, often used for patients with bowel conditions. Ileostomy involves diverting the small intestine's contents through an abdominal stoma, typically for chronic inflammatory diseases. Urostomy is related to urinary diversion, allowing urine to bypass the bladder, usually due to cancer or bladder dysfunction. Each type requires specific products and care solutions for patients.</p></p>
