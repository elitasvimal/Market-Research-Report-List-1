<p><ul><li>RIA</li><li>ELISA</li><li>CLIA</li><li>POCT</li><li>LC-MS/MS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vitamin D testing market comprises various methodologies: RIA (Radioimmunoassay) uses radioactive isotopes for quantification; ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) utilizes enzyme reactions for detection; CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay) employs light emission for measurement; POCT (Point-of-Care Testing) provides rapid results at the test site; and LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) offers high precision and sensitivity in analyzing Vitamin D metabolites. Each method serves different healthcare needs based on accuracy, speed, and accessibility.</p></p>
