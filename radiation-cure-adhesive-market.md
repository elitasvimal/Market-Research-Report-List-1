<p><strong>The Radiation Cure Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Curable Adhesives</li><li>Electron Beam Curable Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation cure adhesive market comprises primarily two types: UV curable adhesives and electron beam curable adhesives. UV curable adhesives utilize ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process, providing fast curing times and suitability for various substrates, particularly in packaging and automotive applications. Electron beam curable adhesives, on the other hand, use high-energy electron beams to achieve rapid curing, offering superior penetration into materials and enhanced durability. Both types are valued for their environmentally friendly, low-VOC formulations and excellent bonding properties.</p></p>
