<p><p>The polysilicon market is divided into two main types: Solar Grade Polysilicon and Electronic Grade Polysilicon. Solar Grade Polysilicon is primarily used in the production of solar panels, requiring lower purity levels (99.9999%) to efficiently convert sunlight into electricity. Electronic Grade Polysilicon, with ultra-high purity (99.9999999%), is essential for manufacturing semiconductors and microchips, where precision and performance are critical. Both types are derived from metallurgical-grade silicon but differ in purification processes and applications, catering to renewable energy and advanced electronics industries, respectively.</p></p>
